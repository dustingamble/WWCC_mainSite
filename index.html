We provide immigrant women and their families with opportunities to improve the quality of their lives through self-development and community action.

WHAT WE DO

Working Women Community Centre is a charitable organization that provides immigrant women with opportunities to improve the quality of their lives, as well as the lives of those in their families and community.

Why We Help

Did you know that in addressing immigrant women and their families, Working Women is addressing the needs of a significant portion of our society.

How We Help

Working Women shows immigrant women that they have the ability to live a fully productive and engaged life and that doing so has a positive effect on those around them.
